Code of Conduct

1. Registration Terms and Conditions

Registration: Must be done via the Worldpackers app.

Contribution: $20 per day per person for operational costs.

Payment System:

  • 50% deposit on the first day.
  • Remaining 50% after half of the stay.
  • Payment via e-banking or cash.

Duration: 1 to 12 weeks.

Minimum Age: 18+ or with parental consent.

Health: Complete health form before arrival.

Attitude and Adaptation: Open to local culture and willing to adapt.

2. Provided Facilities

Accommodation: Traditional local house.

Meals: Three meals a day, prepared by locals.

Transportation:

  • Shuttle service within Senduro.
  • Free pickup from Klakah Station (07:00 - 17:00 WIB).

Nature Tourism: Eco-tourism and cultural experiences.

Certificate: E-Certificate upon completion.

Social Network: Connect with local and international volunteers.

3. Program Schedule

Day One: Introduction and Adaptation

  • Program briefing and rules introduction.
  • Meet locals and learn about culture.
  • Discuss program and align goals.
  • Rest and recover from the journey.

Day Two and Beyond: Flexible Schedule

  • Activities based on ongoing projects.
  • 4 flexible working hours per day.
4. Volunteer Responsibilities
  • Respecting Culture: Honor local traditions and norms.
  • Active Participation: Engage in program activities.
  • Cleanliness & Environment: Maintain hygiene and sustainability.
  • Behavior Ethics: Avoid harming local communities.
  • Reporting: Report issues to the coordinator.
5. Prohibited Activities
  • Prohibited Substances: No alcohol, narcotics, or illegal substances.
  • Discrimination & Violence: Zero tolerance.
  • Environmental Damage: No harming flora or fauna.
  • Leaving the Area: No leaving without permission.
6. Sanctions and Consequences
  • Minor Violations: Verbal warning.
  • Major Violations: Expulsion without refund.
  • Legal Action: Serious cases reported to authorities.
