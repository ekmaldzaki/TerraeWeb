Welcome to Terrae!

Terrae is an ecotourism destination near Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park, offering an immersive cultural experience with remote village communities in the tropical forest. Focusing on local product processing, natural destination promotion, and sustainable ecotourism projects, Terrae involves a core team and local residents as tour guides, food providers, and transportation service providers. Committed to creating job opportunities and preserving nature, Terrae is certified in tourism management by the Programma Uitzending Managers (PUM) Netherlands, ensuring a welcoming and memorable travel experience with a touch of Javanese culture.

Our Projects

At Terrae, we empower local communities through sustainable ecotourism, aligning with global Sustainable Development Goals. Our projects create jobs, support community resilience, and promote environmental conservation. By involving local residents in tourism, we help reduce poverty (SDG 1), foster sustainable communities (SDG 11), and contribute to climate action (SDG 13).

Explore our initiatives and be part of the change!

Ecotourism

Terrae is an ecotourism destination that offers a cultural immersion experience with remote village communities in the tropical forests near Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park. Tourists can enjoy the beauty of nature, explore various destinations such as waterfalls and mountains, and experience an authentic local lifestyle. Additionally, Terrae also focuses on processing local products as part of a sustainable tourism experience.

Environment

As part of its commitment to sustainability, Terrae promotes environmental conservation through various ecotourism programs. By maintaining a balance between tourism and nature, Terrae supports responsible natural resource management and encourages eco-friendly practices. Through these initiatives, Terrae strives to preserve the surrounding ecosystem and raise awareness of the importance of conservation.

Social

Terrae is not just a tourist destination but also a platform for empowering local communities. By involving residents as tour guides, food providers, and transportation service operators, Terrae creates job opportunities and improves the economic well-being of the surrounding community. Additionally, Terrae has received a tourism management certification from the Netherlands' Programma Uitzending Managers in recognition of its contribution to the development of sustainable and culturally-based village tourism in Java.
